Ramnagr

Ramnagar, Uttarakhand is famous as the "gateway" to Jim Corbett National Park, India's oldest national park. It's a popular destination for wildlife enthusiasts due to its proximity to the park and the opportunity for safaris and birdwatching. Additionally, Ramnagar offers a blend of natural beauty, cultural experiences, and spiritual sites like the Hanuman Dham.

Things to do in Ramnagar

  • wildlife safaris
  • visiting the Garjiya Devi Temple
  • exploring the Corbett Museum
