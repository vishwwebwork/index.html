nainital

Nainital (NTL), located in the Kumaon region of Uttarakhand, is renowned for its beautiful Naini Lake, making it a popular tourist destination. It's also known for its colonial heritage and educational institutions. Additionally, Naini Lake is believed to be one of the 51 Shakti Peeths, adding to its spiritual significance.

  • visiting the Naina Devi Temple
